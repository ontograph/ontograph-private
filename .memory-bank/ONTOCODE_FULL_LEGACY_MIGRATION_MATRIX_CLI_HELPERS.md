## Review Notes

- Do not rename internal fields such as `codex_self_exe`, `codex_linux_sandbox_exe`, or `main_execve_wrapper_exe` in the first helper cutover unless OntoIndex impact proves the blast radius is bounded.
- Do not remove `CODEX_*` env/state compatibility in CLI/helper stages; that belongs to Stage 5.
- Do not rename npm/Python package identities, release asset names, generated protocol names, or telemetry names in this matrix; those belong to other Stage 0 matrices and later release-owner decisions.
- Treat Linux sandbox argv0 behavior as runtime-critical: tests must exercise actual dispatch/re-entry behavior, not only manifest names.
- Treat app-server and TUI as consumers of helper/runtime values, not owners of helper naming policy.
